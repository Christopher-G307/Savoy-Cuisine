Welcome To Savoy Cuisine

Savoy Cuisine - Five Star Restaurant & Deli: Family Friendly Restaurant in Kemptville Whether you’re looking to switch up your dinner routine by ordering takeout, treat yourself and your friends out to brunch on the weekend, or simply enjoy a dinner out with friends, come visit our family owned and operated restaurant in Kemptville—proud recipient of a TripAdvisor award. For over 25 years, Five Star Restaurant & Deli has been serving our community and surrounding areas with tasty specialities like Chinese food, Italian food, pizza, and smoked meats. You’ll have plenty to choose from our diverse menu filled with lots of different flavours and dishes, including a special breakfast menu. As a fully licensed establishment, we offer all domestic beer brands as well as various wine and spirits that are perfect at the end of a long day; we also carry Pepsi products.
